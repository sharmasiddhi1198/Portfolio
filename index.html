The problem I solve

Most teams don't have a data problem, an automation problem, or a GenAI problem in isolation — they have a translation problem. The systems, the numbers, and the manual steps already exist. What's missing is someone who can turn them into a decision, a workflow, or a working prototype.

My approach

Scope the real question with stakeholders. Get hands-on with the data — SQL, dashboards, pipelines. Automate the repetitive parts with Alteryx or n8n. Use GenAI to accelerate analysis and prototyping where it genuinely helps. The output is never just a report — it's a dashboard someone uses, a workflow that runs without me, or a product concept someone can evaluate.
